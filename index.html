Dear artblox curators. Here we tried to collect together all the information about us and about our project. Happy reading!

About Us

We are two ordinary guys graduating from a regular high school. Yes, we do not have any creative history, we are not professional artists, but we have goals, dreams and a desire to make this world a better place. We really believe that our project deserves the right to exist.

About the project

Let's not hide, the idea of the project was borrowed from MurakamiFlowers. The project will include about 100-300 unique generative 3d arts. We would like to create a small circulation project to support the protection of nature and the ocean from pollution.

About the project

We are doing this project not for the sake of revenue, but for the sake of personal interest, in order to benefit this world. Therefore, we will transfer half of the proceeds to independent charitable foundations involved in cleaning up the world's oceans and coastal areas, and we will donate the other half to the study of hydrogen energy.

Conclusion

Yes, our art is not distinguished by an idea or a special pictorial structure, there are no influential people behind our project, and we ourselves are not known to anyone. We are ordinary guys dreaming of bringing our little project to life. We would be very grateful if you would support us
